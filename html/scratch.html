Bodybuilding


Main Reasons Everyone Should Start Working Out:

  1. Improves overall physical health
    • Studies have shown that even working out for a couple hours a week improves one's lifespan and quality of life
    • Enables carry-over of physical skills into other sports and activities
  2. Improves mental health
    • The gym can serve as an outlet for many people
    • Workouts can be meditative
    • Helps boost self-confidence
  3. Improves aesthetic appearance
    • While not everyone's goal in the gym, it can help with the aesthetic appearance of one's muscles
  4. Forms connections in and out of the gym
    • Forming connections through simple interactions is relatively common in the gym
    • Even outside the gym, being able to relate to other gym-goers provides a solid foundation for conversation

My Motivation: Chris Bumstead
